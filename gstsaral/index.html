Not Professional Advice: This app is just a mathematical calculator tool. It does not provide legal, tax, accounting, or financial advice. Please verify your bills with a professional Chartered Accountant (CA) before final use.

Privacy Policy

Your Data is Safe: We do not ask for any sign-up, email, phone number, or account creation. There is no cloud sync and we do not have a centralized data server.
